About me

Described by his peers as New Zealand’s “pop prince,” Chris Bates is a rising singer-songwriter celebrated for his soulful vocals and emotionally charged storytelling.

With singles like November (No Hard Feelings), Edinburgh, and Takeaway Cup, Bates has carved a distinct place in New Zealand’s pop scene, earning spots on Spotify’s New Music Friday AU/NZ, Peaceful Pop, and Apple Music’s New In Pop. His latest single, Suitcase—the first taste of his upcoming release—captures the raw, bittersweet emotions of a relationship ending as one person prepares to leave the country.
